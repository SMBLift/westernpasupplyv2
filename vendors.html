Fairborn Cement Company

Fairborn Cement Company is an American-owned cement producer based in Ohio and a subsidiary of Eagle Materials. They manufacture portland cement and a full line of masonry cements marketed under the MIAMI trade name, including grey masonry cement (Types N, S, and M), MIAMI mortar cement, and MIAMICOLOR colored masonry cement available in 44 colors.

Key Product Lines

Portland Cement MIAMI Masonry Cement MIAMI Mortar Cement MIAMICOLOR (44 Colors)
Authorized Dealer

Superior Clay Products

Superior Clay Corporation manufactures quality handcrafted clay products for custom fireplaces, chimneys, and wood-fired ovens. Their product line includes Rumford fireplace components, over 60 styles of decorative chimney pots, herringbone fireboxes, brick masonry bread ovens, flue liners, and architectural terra cotta. Based in Uhrichsville, Ohio.

Key Product Lines

Rumford Fireplace Components Chimney Pots (60+ Styles) Flue Liners Bread Ovens & Terra Cotta
Authorized Dealer

Equiparts

Equiparts is a Pittsburgh-area plumbing and maintenance supply company headquartered in Oakmont, PA. In business since 1968, they are a master distributor for major brands including Sloan Valve, Chicago Faucets, Moen, Toto, T&S Brass, Bobrick, and more. They specialize in hard-to-find repair parts for commercial and institutional facilities. Employee-owned since 2024.

Key Product Lines

Plumbing Repair Parts Commercial Fixtures Sloan & Chicago Faucets Maintenance Supplies
Authorized Dealer

Bon Tool

Bon Tool is a leading manufacturer of professional-grade hand tools and equipment for the trowel trades, headquartered in Gibsonia, PA. With over 6,000 products, they serve masonry, concrete, drywall, flooring, stucco, plastering, hardscape, and general construction professionals. A third-generation family company recognized globally for innovation, value, and service.

Key Product Lines

Masonry & Concrete Tools Drywall & Plastering Tools Flooring & Hardscape Tools 6,000+ SKUs
Authorized Dealer

Do It Best Lumber

Do It Best is one of the largest hardware cooperatives in the United States, headquartered in Fort Wayne, Indiana. As a member-owned co-op, they provide independent retailers with access to over 67,000 products including lumber, building materials, hardware, and home improvement supplies at competitive pricing with world-class distribution.
