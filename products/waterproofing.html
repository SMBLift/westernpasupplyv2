Waterproofing

Keep Water Out. Keep Structures Sound.

What We Carry

Pittsburgh's climate demands reliable waterproofing. Western PA Supply carries a full range of products for foundations, basements, decks, and structural applications. From sheet membranes to liquid-applied coatings, we have what you need.

Foundation Waterproofing

Below-grade waterproofing membranes and coatings for new construction and remediation.

Drainage Products

Drainage boards, drain tile, and interior drainage systems.

Sheet Membranes

Self-adhering and torch-applied waterproofing membranes.

Liquid-Applied Membranes

Spray and roller-applied waterproofing for irregular surfaces.

Dampproofing

Asphalt-based and cementitious dampproofing for below-grade walls.

Water Stops

PVC and rubber water stops for concrete joints.
