Plumbing

Reliable Plumbing Products for Every Project

What We Carry

Western PA Supply stocks a full range of plumbing products through our True Value network. From copper and PVC pipe to fixtures and supply lines, we have what you need for new construction, renovation, and repair projects.

Pipes & Tubing

PVC, CPVC, copper, PEX, and ABS pipe in standard lengths and diameters.

Fittings

Elbows, tees, couplings, adapters, and specialty fittings in all common materials.

Valves

Ball valves, gate valves, check valves, and shut-off valves for supply and drain lines.

Fixtures

Faucets, sinks, toilets, and plumbing fixtures for residential and commercial use.

Supply Lines & Connectors

Braided and rigid supply lines, compression fittings, and quick-connect systems.

Drain & Waste

Drain assemblies, P-traps, waste fittings, and sewage components.
