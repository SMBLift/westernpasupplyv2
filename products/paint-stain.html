Paint & Stain

Color Your World. Protect Your Surfaces.

Western PA Supply offers a complete selection of paint, stain, and coating products through our True Value partnership. From premium interior paints to industrial-grade exterior coatings, we have the products and expertise to help you choose the right finish for every project.

Interior Paint

Premium latex and acrylic paints in a full range of colors and sheens for walls, ceilings, trim, and cabinetry.

Exterior Paint

Weather-resistant paints and coatings for siding, trim, and outdoor surfaces that stand up to Pittsburgh's climate.

Stains & Finishes

Wood stains, deck stains, and clear finishes for interior and exterior wood surfaces, enhancing natural beauty and protection.

Primers & Sealers

Bonding primers, stain-blocking primers, and specialty sealers for proper surface preparation and lasting paint adhesion.

Brushes, Rollers & Supplies

Professional applicators, trays, tape, drop cloths, and prep supplies for clean, efficient painting results.

Specialty Coatings

Floor paint, garage coatings, rust preventives, and specialty finishes for demanding commercial and residential applications.
