Chimney & Fireplace

Quality Products for Safe, Beautiful Hearths

As an authorized Superior Clay Products dealer, Western PA Supply offers a premium selection of chimney and fireplace products. From clay chimney liners to complete Rumford fireplace systems, we carry trusted products backed by over a century of manufacturing expertise.

Clay Chimney Liners

Superior Clay round and rectangular flue liners for safe, efficient chimney construction.

Rumford Fireplaces

Complete Rumford fireplace systems known for their superior heat output and timeless design.

Fireplace Components

Fireboxes, throat pieces, and smoke chambers for fireplace construction.

Dampers

Top-mount and throat dampers for chimney draft control and energy efficiency.

Chimney Caps

Protective caps to keep out rain, debris, and animals while improving draft.

Flue Accessories

Thimbles, clean-out doors, and specialty flue components.
