Concrete & Masonry

The Foundation of Every Great Project

Western PA Supply carries a full range of concrete and masonry products for projects of every size. Whether you're pouring a foundation, building a retaining wall, or laying brick, we have the materials and expertise to help you get the job done right.

Concrete Mix

Ready-mix concrete in various strengths for foundations, footings, posts, and general use.

Mortar & Cement

Portland cement, masonry cement, and pre-mixed mortars for block, brick, and stone work.

Block & Brick

Concrete blocks, bricks, and specialty masonry units for structural and decorative applications.

Natural & Manufactured Stone

Stone veneer, flagstone, and manufactured stone products for walls, facades, and hardscaping.

Stucco & Plaster

Stucco base coats, finish coats, and plastering supplies.

Rebar & Mesh

Reinforcing steel, wire mesh, and accessories for concrete reinforcement.

Forming Supplies

Concrete forms, stakes, ties, and accessories for shaping poured concrete.
