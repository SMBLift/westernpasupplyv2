Joint Sealant & Expansion

Seal Every Joint. Protect Every Surface.

Proper joint sealing is critical to the performance and longevity of concrete, masonry, and building envelopes. Western PA Supply stocks a full range of sealants, caulks, backer rod, and expansion joint materials for commercial and residential applications.

Construction Sealants

Polyurethane, silicone, and hybrid sealants for structural and weatherproofing joints in commercial and residential buildings.

Caulking

Acrylic, latex, and specialty caulks for interior and exterior applications, from trim work to weatherization.

Backer Rod

Closed-cell and open-cell backer rod in various diameters for proper joint preparation and sealant depth control.

Expansion Joints

Pre-formed expansion joint filler for concrete slabs, walls, and pavement to accommodate movement and prevent cracking.

Joint Fillers

Self-leveling and non-sag joint fillers for horizontal and vertical applications in concrete and masonry construction.

Tapes & Primers

Joint tapes, sealant primers, and surface preparation products to ensure proper adhesion and long-lasting performance.
