Tools & Equipment

The Right Tools for Every Job.

What We Carry

From professional-grade concrete tools to everyday hand tools, Western PA Supply has what you need. As a True Value Member Store, we offer an extensive selection of quality tools and equipment at competitive prices.

Hand Tools

Hammers, levels, tape measures, pliers, screwdrivers, and essential trade tools.

Power Tools

Drills, saws, grinders, rotary hammers, demolition tools, and corded/cordless power tool systems.

Concrete & Masonry Tools

Trowels, floats, edgers, jointers, mixers, and specialty concrete finishing tools.

Measuring & Layout

Laser levels, chalk lines, squares, and precision measuring tools.

Cutting Tools

Blades, bits, hole saws, and abrasive cutting wheels.

Safety Equipment

Hard hats, gloves, eye protection, hearing protection, and high-vis gear.

Roofing Tools

Pry bars, roofing hammers, shingle removers, utility knives, caulk guns, and nail guns.

Scaffolding & Jobsite Equipment

Sawhorses, work platforms, ladders, scaffolding, and jobsite accessories.

Caulking & Application Tools

Caulk guns, grout bags, tuck pointers, application wands, and dispensing tools.

Tool Storage & Accessories

Tool bags, belts, buckets, organizers, and jobsite boxes.
