About Western PA Supply

Locally Owned. Nationally Connected. Pittsburgh Proud.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Western PA Supply Co is a locally owned building and construction supply company serving the Greater Pittsburgh area. We opened our doors with a commitment to providing the products, pricing, and service that contractors, builders, and homeowners in this region deserve.

As a True Value Member Store, we combine the buying power and product depth of a national hardware cooperative with the personalized service and expertise of a local supplier. That means you get competitive wholesale pricing on thousands of products, backed by a team that knows your name and understands your project.

Western PA Supply was built on a simple idea: Pittsburgh's contractors, builders, and homeowners deserve a supply partner that understands their needs, carries the right products, and provides genuine expertise. Whether you are framing a new build, repointing a chimney, or tackling a weekend project, we are here to make sure you have what you need to get it done right.
